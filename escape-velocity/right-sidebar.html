Welcome to Parivartan

You will never be the same again.

PARIVARTAN is an upcoming ISKCON Youth Project at NIT, Calicut, Kerala. The Project is intended to impart Krsna Consciousness to the bright young students of NIT in a modern and applicative fashion.

It is inspired by the teachings of His Divine Grace A C Bhakti Vedanta Swami Prabhupada. The project is steered under the guidance of HH Jayapataka Swami Maharaja and conducted under the supervision of HH Bhakti Vinoda Swami Maharaja.

Few alumni of NIT Calicut have come forward and generously contributed towards this noble cause. With that the foundation for the institution has been completed and structural work for ground floor is in progress. Some of the recent photographs have been shown here.
